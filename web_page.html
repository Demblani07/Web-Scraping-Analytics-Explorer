Buyer's Guide

The potential of big data

Humans are producing mammoth proportions of data every second. The world would generate 44 zettabytes (44 billion terabytes) annually by 2020.

Most of the data which is generated in the world is unstructured data (emails, social media posts, videos, photos, audio files, webpages). This data if used effectively has the potential to provide invaluable insights to your business. By using various Big Data Systems like Hadoop, MongoDB and Spark Big Data Companies will sift out valuable information from the huge amounts of unstructured data that your business generates.

Businesses understand the value of Big Data Analytics in improving the profit margins, the number of companies offering Big Data services has also increased rapidly.

The market of BDaaS(Big data as a service) has grown phenomenally and new platforms are emerging that are providing valuable Big Data Consulting Services to businesses all over the world. 

Things to look while selecting a Big Data Analytics company

Many companies claim to be efficient Big Data Solution Providers. But in order to separate the wheat from the chaff, you will need to judge the level of expertise of Big Data Analytics Companies based on various factors.

Range of services offered
There are various services involved in Big data, like Big Data consulting, Data Acquisition, Data Integration, Data warehousing, Data modeling, Data Visualization, and Data Integration. It is not required that your Big Data Service Provider should be well equipped to provide all these services. So, you must first analyze the services that your project shall require and accordingly see whether your Big Data Company is providing the requisite services or not.

Types of Big Data projects completed
Ensure that your Big Data Analytics Company has the required expertise in providing quality enterprise solution before awarding the contract. The best way to see this is by asking the service provider to provide you with a list of the previous projects completed by them. It would be better if your Big Data Company has previous experience of working on a similar project like yours.

Always ahead of the curve
Big Data analytics is a fast-paced field with new techniques and technologies emerging every day. Your Big Data Consulting Firm should be well acquainted with the latest trends in the ever-growing field of Big Data analytics. This will help your Big Data Company in providing you with the latest Big data solutions always.

Integration with the existing IT infrastructure
Ask your Big Data Vendor, whether the system they propose would be able to gel well with the existing IT infrastructure of your company. Many Big Data Analytics Service Providers will insist on making changes in your systems. Your employees will need to re-train according to the new system. The integration should be such that it provides a hassle process for the workforce to make or add the required adjustments to the data.

Make a cost-benefit analysis of installing the new system. Efficient Big Data Companies will have systems that will integrate easily with your existing infrastructure without much fuss.

Versatility in scaling up/down
A Good Big Data Analytics firm will be able to provide you with a solution that can scale up according to your needs. Understand that as your firm grows, so does the data inflow and you will require a Big Data Analytics system that can scale up according to your needs easily. Many experienced top Big Data Analytics Companies will provide you with a highly versatile system that can easily handle large spikes in data inflow.

Availability of Data Visualization tools
Big Data should be presented in an easily digestible manner. Data visualization tools play an important role in ensuring this. Ask your Big Data Analytics Company the kind of data visualization tools they provide. Discuss with your Big Data Consulting Firm whether they can customize the visualization tools according to your needs. The data visualization tools will enable your employees to gather the information they need in a much faster manner.

Robust Data Security
While choosing from the list of the best Big Data Analytics Companies, bear in mind that your vendor would have a lot of sensitive information about your business such as the information that can potentially have a negative impact on your business if it gets in the wrong hands. Hence, it is important that your Big Data Company has all the required set of security protocols in place to avoid any mishap.
